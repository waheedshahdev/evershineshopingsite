# Evershine Shopping

**Evershine Shopping** is a feature-rich **eCommerce platform** developed in **CodeIgniter** and **MySQL**, designed for boutiques and fashion retailers in Pakistan that specialize in **unstitched suits**.  
The platform enables customers to explore collections, customize their fabric orders, and place purchases online, while providing administrators with full control over inventory, orders, and payments.
